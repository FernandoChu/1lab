The base change functor is a right adjoint. We construct the left adjoint directly, then give the unit and counit, and finally prove the triangle identities.

The left adjoint, called dependent sum and written ‚àëf\sum_f‚àëf‚Äã, is given on objects by precomposition with fff, and on morphisms by what is essentially the identity function ‚Äî only the witness of commutativity must change.

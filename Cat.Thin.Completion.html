Poset completionüîó

We construct a universal completion of a proset to a poset. Initially, recall the terms. A proset (which is how we refer to strict, thin categories) is a set equipped with a relation ‚àí‚â§‚àí- \le -‚àí‚â§‚àí which is both reflexive and transitive, but not necessarily antisymmetric. There is a functor between the underlying category of a proset C\ca{C}C and the underlying category of its completion C^\widehat{\ca{C}}C, with object part given by the quotient map inc.

         ‚Üí Functor (X .underlying) (Poset-completion X .underlying)

This functor has morphism part given by the identity function, so it‚Äôs fully faithful. It exhibits C\ca{C}C as a full subproset of C^\widehat{\ca{C}}C.
